3266012   応用ドイツ語作文B

1.0 単位, 2 - 4 年次, 春ABC 木4

授業概要

ドイツ語の様々な必要に応じた作文能力を養成する。CEFR(ヨーロッパ共通参照枠)のB1レベルに到達することを目標とする。

備考

全学群対象 選択必修クラスの単位に代替することはできない。原則、「ドイツ語基礎」A及びBの履修者に限る。
2022年度開講せず。
G科目

授業方法

演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

1. Ability to communicate in written and spoken form, to interact, to mediate, and to work in groups.
2. Learning to learn (e.g. loci method); note taking.
3. Critical thinking.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

In class, we will be working on our writing skills (but also on related skills), often in groups by using various tools (e.g. Google Slides). Inbetween classes, the  learner diary will serve as a tool for the cycles of writing-feedback-revising, transparent to all course participants. The goal is to reach B1 level for the writing skill.

キーワード

Individual writing, cooperative writing, peer feedback, learner diary.

授業計画

In principal, each class starts with a quiz on listening & reading comprehension, on structures learned in class so far (translation, Q&A). Various online tools will be employed (e.g. Manaba, Zoom, Flipgrid, Google Slides, Teams, etc.).

第1講 Introduction of the course; short writing  assignment. Part 1 of a model  test A2 or B1 (participants' individual choice)
第2講 Feedback on model test. Fairy tale "Die Bremer Stadtmusikanten";  Reading of a fairy tale in "Leichter Sprache" in breakout rooms.
第3講 Writing exercise: Gap filling and solving scrambled text portions on key structures (e.g. from fairy tale, model tests)
第4講 Collaborative writing of a fairy tale or of a topic of own choice with Google Slides. This tool allows continuous feedback. Hints for test 1.
第5講 1st writing test: Part of a model test, either A2 or B1, according to participants' individual choice.
第6講 Feedback on the test and texts. Semi-formal writing of an email, individually or in a pair.
第7講 Feedback on emails. Collection of typical phrases and structures.
第8講 Semi-formal writing of a short text on a topic of choice, individually or in a group.
第9講 Feedback on texts. Collection of typical phrases and structures.
第10講 2nd writing test (either a part of an A2 or B1 model test).
第11講 Plenary feedback on selected portions of the written texts. Collection of key vocabulary and key sentences.
第12講 Creatiive writing, individually or in groups, in Google Slides with continuous feedback.
第13講 Review of structures and hints for writing from the course, in particular as a preparation for the 3rd test.
第14講 3rd test: Complete written part of a model test,  either A1: a formal email. B2: A written contribution to a discussion on a given topic.
第15講 Feedback on final test. Reading aloud texts and language structures from the course for better retention.

The lesson plan might be adapted due to the progression of the lessons and unforeseen circumstances.

成績評価方法

Homework (learner diary, question time, notes) 50%, 3 tests (3 x 10%). Writing in class: 10%. Final report: 10%. In writing, effort and critical thinking counts more error-free productions.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

About 1 h self-study per lesson outside of class is recommended. Using mnemonic methods (e.g. for memorizing items instantly) can reduce this time or improve the outcome. Learning together with classmates can increase the joy of learning AND improve the outcome.
