BE21371   Introduction to Law

2.0 単位, 1・2 年次, 春AB 金3; 春C 水3,4
宮坂 渉, 秋山 肇

授業概要

This course aims to make students get comprehensive introduction to Jurisprudence or legal studies. It addresses the character and contents of each field in Jurisprudence through practical and case examples.

備考

The number of students is limited to 25.
英語で授業。
対面

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

This course is related to General-propose competence "4.Broad perspective and internationalism".
This course is related to Special competence "1.Understanding international relations" and "6.Cross-cultural awareness skills".

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

By taking this course, students are expected to learn the basic concepts of law and their function in our international society needed for understanding international relations from a broad perspective and for enhancing skills for cross-cultural communications.

キーワード

Jurisprudence, legal studies, constitution, criminal law, labor law, Property, torts, contracts, family law, lawyers, tribunal decisions.

履修条件

The number of students is limited to 30-35. This course is designed for students of College of Social Sciences and International Studies, so priority upon registration will be given to them.

成績評価方法

The number of students who can acquire grade A+ or A is limited to 30% of students registered.

1st to 10th session:
Items and percentage of grading are following:
Weekly Assignment (Questions and/or Comments to the reading materials): 60%
Research Paper: 40%
Note: Whether the questions or comments were dealt with in the classroom or not has no effect on the grading.

11th to 20th session:
Weekly Assignment (Questions and/or Comments to the presentation and further research): 60%
Presentation: 40%
