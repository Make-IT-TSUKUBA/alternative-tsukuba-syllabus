授業概要

修士・博士課程の学生は、ドイツやイタリアなどの西欧諸国だけでなく、旧東ドイツなどの東欧諸国やインドなどの南欧諸国における最近の国家的・地域的・地域的背景を知ることで、現代の「グローバル世界」において遺産とアイデンティティがどのように関連しているかを学ぶ。遺産保存の分野では、市民参加に根ざした実践は、特に1980年代以降、特定の地域的・政治的文脈によって、多くの成果を上げてきた。しかし、実利主義的な学術的言説は、国や地域の文脈を無視する傾向があり、「遺産化」や「組織化」に伴う対立も生じている。本講演では、30年以上にわたって実践的、理論的、そして学術的な見解に基づいて遺産学という学問分野を発展させてきたLeo Schmidt教授，Alexsandra Skedzuhn-Safir教授（いずれもドイツBTU），Jens Casper教授ら（エアフルト応用科学大学）の協力を得て、オンライン上でさらに学術的な議論を展開する。

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Master's and PhD students can learn about how heritage and identity are related in the contemporary 'global world' by learning about the recent national, regional and local context in Western European countries such as Germany and Italy, but also in Eastern European countries such as the former East Germany and Southern European countries such as India. The project can be They will also be able to participate in further academic discussions and broaden their perspectives online with the help of German academics and practitioners from developed countries in cultural heritage.

Topic of the year 2023 (Winter Semester 2023)
"Destruction, conservation or reconstruction - Towards new cultural heritage studies based on the theory and experiences within architectural conservation studies in Germany"

Students of Master and Doctoral degree programs will learn, how heritage and identity-related in the modern “global world”, by knowing the recent national, regional, and local context, not only in Western European countries but also in Eastern European countries (such as in former Eastern Germany). In the field of heritage preservation, practices rooted in civic engagement had much dedicated due to specific regional and political contexts, especially since the 1980s. Although, pragmatic academic discourses tend to ignore the national or regional context and conflicts emerged along with Heritazation(遺産化,isan-ka)" or "Institutionalisation(組織化,soshiki-ka)". This lecture will be assisted by Prof. Dr. Leo Schmidt, Dr. Alexandra Skedzuhn-Safir and other pragmatic conservators who are actively engaged to adoptive reuses in Germany who has over thirty years developed the discipline of heritage studies base on practical, theoretical as well as academic views, to extend the further academic discussions online.

成績評価方法

The degree of independent participation and results of group work in class (100%) will be evaluated based on the level of understanding of the content, etc. presented in class and the degree of achievement in meeting the class objectives, while checking against the grading criteria in Degree P. In cases where attendance does not reach 70% of the total number of students, the presentation is not subject to grading.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

This subject is set up as a one-credit course, with a total of 15 hours of lessons, but 15 hours' worth of study is required outside of class for review and preparation, writing reports, etc.
Outside of class, students should review the content of each lecture, understand the meaning of technical terms, etc., and collect the latest information on the themes and events covered in class from news, academic journals, etc.
