0AQT047   環境影響評価論

3.0 単位, 2 年次, 秋ABC 応談
前田 義昌, 鈴木 石根, 辻村 真貴

授業概要

本コースでは妥当な環境の意思決定に不可欠なツールとしての環境アセスメント(EIA)の方法論を紹介し、概念、方法、課題、EIAプロセスの様々な段階の概論を提供する。検討はEIAプロセスの様々な段階、例えばスクリーニング、スコーピング、EIA文書準備、市民の参加、レビューとアセスメント、モニタリングと監査、控訴権と意思決定などで行う。また、空気、騒音、水、廃棄物管理、環境リスク、生態学的影響、社会経済影響評価など、EIA報告書の構成要素についても分析する。環境関連の法律や環境管理、市民参加の重要性なども討論する。さらにマレーシアの事例研究を利用して、日本のEIAとの比較を行う。

授業計画

Week 1 Introduction to Environmental Impact Assessment
The need for EIA, considerations prior to EIA, Legal Requirement, Projects subjected to EIA, who can conduct EIA, Approving Authorities.

Week 2 The EIA Procedure
The principal stages of the EIA, timing of environmental input, Preliminary EIA and Detailed EIA

Week 3 The Review Process
Time taken for the EIA Review, How the EIA reports are processed.

Week 4 Screening and scoping
Screening of project sites by considering environmental, engineering and economic aspects. Scoping at an early stage in the process and is designed to ensure that the environmental studies provide all the relevant information on the impacts of the project and the alternatives to the project.

Week 5 The EIA Report -The Environmental studies
Air, noise, water, waste management, environmental risk,
ecological impact, and socio-economic impact during the project and the impact to the environment.

Week 6 - 7 Case study

Week 8 Mid-Summary

Week 9 Mitigation Steps
Identification of major impacts and steps to mitigate or minimize the effects

Week 10 Involvement of stake holders and community
Community consultation, Social impact assessment and legal requirement

Week 11 Environmental Management Plan
Detailed planning by the proponent to ensure the environmental impacts are minimized during the project

Week 12-15 Site visit & audit

Final Examination
