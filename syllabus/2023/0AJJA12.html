0AJJA12   Physics of Optoelectronic Devices

1.0 単位, 1・2 年次, 春AB 水5
末益 崇, 蓮沼 隆

授業概要

太陽電池を中心とする受光素子の理解に必要な光照射時の半導体中のキャリアダイナミクスを実空間とk空間で理解し、 キャリア密度分布および電流電圧特性を導出する。また、 ダンデム型太陽電池、集光型太陽電池など、エネルギー変換効率が30%を超える超高効率太陽電池についても、最先端の研究動向を紹介する。

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

(1) Understanding general physics related to photon emission and absorption in semiconductors based on quantum mechanics.

(2) Capable of understanding the impact of localized states within the band gap on the optical properties of semiconductors.

(3) Capable of understanding the mechanism of carrier transport by diffusion, electric field, and others.

(4) Knowing the operation principles of various kinds of solar cells.

(5) Capable of designing a tandem-structure solar cell.

(6) Capable of explaining the condition for high efficiency solar cells.

(7) Capable of explaining the present status and future prospects of solar cells.

キーワード

Optical transition of electrons, SRH recombination statistics, carrier transport, solar cell, passivation.

授業計画

This course will be conducted in English, but Japanese students are really welcome.
It consists of 10 sessions, and each session is given as follows:

Session 1: General background of solar cells

Session 2: Absorption of photon energy by electrons  I - derivation of Fermi's golden rule

Session 3: Absorption of photon energy by electrons II - derivation of time-evolution of the squared magnitude of wavefunction

Session 4: Carrier transport properties

Session 5: Carrier distribution in a pn junction diode under a small forward bias voltage

Session 6: Current-voltage characteristics of a homojunction diode under dark

Session 7: Current-voltage characteristics of a homojunction diode under illumination

Session 8: Factors degrading the solar cell performance I - carrier recombination via localized states within the band gap

Session 9: Factors degrading the solar cell performance II - series & shunt resistance, surface recombination, and urbach tails

Session 10: Present status and future prospects of solar cells
