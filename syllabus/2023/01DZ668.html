授業概要

This course covers some topics in intermediate microeconomics. The topics include price-discrimination, decision theory under risk, game theory (strategic interaction), oligopoly market, and asymmetric information (moral hazard and adverse selection). Apart from the basic competitive market model, we will study other aspects of markets such as monopolies , duopolies. Introductory calculus is used in this course but the required skill is rudimentary.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

In the real economic world, we observe a lot of uncertainty and imperfect competition. This class will consider these market failures using game theory and other techniques, assuming a basic knowledge of microeconomics, which deals with competitive markets.

キーワード

Expected utility, Arrow-Debreu equilibrium, Game theory, Mechanism design
