授業概要

この講義では, ディスカッション, ケーススタディ形式も組み合わせ, 既存の事業を変革あるいは革新したり, 新たな事業を創造していくために重要となる方法論について学んでいく. その際に, 顧客からの視点と, 業界のバリューチェーンの双方の視点から事業を理解することを重視し, 創造・変革について議論を行っていく. さらに, この講義では, サプライチェーン・マネジメント, ビジネスエコシステム, およびシステム思考, イノベイティブ発想などの基本的概念についても触れ, 受講生の起業家力の強化を目指す.

Types of competencies expected to be gained after taking this course

At this course, we will examine how to innovate a business and transform it from both customer view point and industry value chain view point. This course will cover the basics concept of SCM, Business Eco System, and system/innovative thinking, aiming the enhancement of the entrepreneurship capability of attendants.

Course Objectives(Learning Outcomes)

Creating innovation, new business, and products/services are very critical for the success of both start-ups and established firms. In order to do so, you should have a customer-centric view point, create a unique value to customer, and develop the system to deliver it.
In this course, we are going to examine multidisciplinary perspectives, which are useful for value creation. At the first class, we will discuss a specific case to grasp the importance of business system/or conduct a business game (based on the class delivery method (online or offline). Then, we will cover Demand Chain Management, User Innovation, Technological Transitions, Ecosystem, and Servitization. At latter half of this course, we will discuss several ways of thinking including system thinking. Students are expected to read case assignments and distributed papers be-
fore/after the class and participate actively in class.

Course Keywords

Innovation, Eco system, System thinking

Class Schedule

Class Session 1: Introduction/Case discussion or business game (based on the class delivery method (online or offline). Through the discussion or business game, we make an attempt of understanding the system perspective of business.

Class Session 2: Demand Chain Management: We will review input-output sys- tem from customer-centric viewpoint. We use several business cases for class discussion. We will also discuss the importance of users for innovation.

Class Session 3: Ecosystem and Servilization: We mainly focus on understanding the mechanism of socio-technological regime transitions, which is very important to new value creation. We also review what “service” is. Open Innovation andFive Strategy Rules are mentioned briefly.

Class Session 4: Entrepreneur Thinking/System Thinking: We will change the topic from business perspectives toward the way of thinking. We will discuss the hint for innovative idea generation and will examine how to deepen your thoughts

Class Session 5: What is a Corporation: New business idea is realized with the form of “Corporation” in most situations. We will discuss the essence of corporation and management in order to enhance your entrepreneurship/leadership mentality.
