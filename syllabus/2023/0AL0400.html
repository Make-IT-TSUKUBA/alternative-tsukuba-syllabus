授業の到達目標(学修成果)

The collection and analysis of data through experiments is one of the cornerstones of the scientific method. In this course, we study the general philosophy and methods behind experimentalism: Why do we perform experiments, what is a good/rigorous experiment, how to plan and design a rigorous experiment, and how to perform statistical analysis on experimental data.

This course is centered around lectures with plenty of examples and study cases. The students will be invited to apply the techniques studied in this lecture to experiment of their own design.

キーワード

Experiment Design, Statistical Testing, statistics, Philosophy of Science, ethics, R Language

授業計画

The course is composed of 10 lectures, described in the list below. These lectures are roughly divided in three big topics: (1) Why and how we do experiments, (2) Techniques of Statistical Inference, and (3) Special cases of statistical inference.

第1週 Course Introduction: What is Science? What is Experimentalism? 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第2週 Statistical Indicators: Means, Variances, Confidence Interval, usage and visualization; 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第3週 Statistical Inference: Introduction to Statistical Testing 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第4週 Lecture Review, Comments on Report 01 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第5週 Statistical Inference: Comparison Testing and Paired Testing 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第6週 Statistical Inference: Non-Normal Data and Multiple Comparisons 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第7週 Statistical Inference: Sample Size Calculation 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第8週 Experiment Design: Block and Factorial Design (Part I) 担当： アランニャ, クラウス
第9週 Experiment Design: Block and Factorial Design (Part II)
第10週 Final Lecture Review, and Discussion on Report 02

成績評価方法

The course will be graded on reports and a Final Exam. The grade proportion is 70% for the reports, and 30% for the exam.

Each report requires the student to plan and execute a simple experiment, and analyze the results, using the techniques discussed in class. The student can choose the topic of the experiment freely, based on their own Master research or daily life and interests. The report is evaluated on the quality of the experimental design, the correctness of the statistical analysis, and the quality of the discussion of the results.

The Final Exam covers the entire material of the course. The students are allowed to bring a reference sheet.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

The students have to execute an experiment, analyze the results and submit the resulting report. It is possible for the student to use data from their own research in this report.
