BE22292   International Financial Institutions and Economic Development in Southeast Asia

2.0 単位, 2 - 4 年次, 秋AB 木3,4
鈴木 英明

授業概要

This course will aim at applying the macroeconomic knowledge to analyze the actual economic development and macroeconomic issues in emerging economies in Southeast Asia on the basis of reports by the World Bank and the IMF.
This course will be conducted in English.

備考

Equivalent to "International Financial Institutions and Economic Development in Emerging Economies in Southeast Asia" (BE22292).
BC12352と同一。
英語で授業。
実務経験教員
対面

授業方法

演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

Students will study  major development and macroeconomic issues in a number of Southeast Asian countries, on the basis of the reports by the World Bank and the IMF.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Students are expected to learn how they could apply their macroeconomic knowledge to the actual economic context in emerging economies.

キーワード

Economic development, macroeconomic management, World Bank, IMF, Vietnam, the Philippines, Indonesia, Thailand

授業計画

1) Analytical framework to discuss economic development (Placement test on macroeconomic knowledge)
2) Analytical framework to discuss macroeconomic issues and financial market trends
3) Development challenges in Vietnam & Thailand
4) Development challenges in Indonesia & the Philippines
5) Recent macroeconomic issues in Vietnam & Thailand
6) Recent macroeconomic issues in Indonesia & the Philippines
7) Financial market trend (Part 1) in Vietnam & Thailand  
8) Financial market trend (Part 1) in Indonesia & the Philippines
9) Financial market trend (Part 2) in Vietnam & Thailand
10) Financial market trend (Part 2) in Indonesia & the Philippines

履修条件

Prerequisite: "International Economics" (BC51071) offered by International Studies Department or its equivalent. In order to participate in this course, students need to have a basic knowledge on the framework of macroeconomic policies discussed in "International Economics" (BC51071). In Session 1, a placement test will be carried out to certify that students have the required knowledge on macroeconomics. Only those students who have demonstrated to have such knowledge in the placement test will be admitted to the class and given course credits.                                                                                                                            Advisories: It is recommended to take BC12342 (Development and Finance) in advance and have knowledge on variety of financing modality for economic development

成績評価方法

Class presentation (40%), class participation  (40%) and final essay (20%). Each student is required to make FOUR class presentations during the period of Session 3 to Session 10.
