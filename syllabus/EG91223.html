授業概要

The goal of this course is to provide experience and background in a variety of field methods used by researchers in geoenvironmental sciences. The course will focus on hands-on field techniques for data gathering (observation, measurement, and others), mapping, and data analysis.

備考

Offered in 2023. This course is offered every 3 years. Permission by teachers.

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

地球学に関する専門的素養,フィールドワーク能力，研究企画・遂行・総括能力

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Participants learn to prepare for field excursions, conduct preliminary research and report their findings, learn several observation methods and apply them on field excursions.

キーワード

human geography, field survey, landscape/land use observation, interview, methodology

授業計画

Students learn human geographical methodology through the following field surveys:

1) A field trip in Aichi, Gifu and Toyama prefectures for 5 days and 4 nights. It will be held in September 2023: it is subject to change if COVID-19 is expanded. Students must join orientations which will start in Spring C term. After the first orientation, students conduct preliminary research to make presentations in the succeeding orientations. For orientations, please sign-up for manaba in advance to make sure that you have access to it. Please check “Course news” and "Resources" on manaba for more information; all required information will be provided before the first orientation begins. After the field trip, students must submit their final report and make their result presentations.

2)Lectures on fieldwork methods in human geography

1) No.1-2 First Orientations
2) No.3 Preliminary research report
3) No.4-5 Lectures and hands-on in the fieldwork methods in human geography
4) No.6-10 Field trip - Day1: observation, record and discussion on the geographical characteristics of target areas
5) No.11-15 Field trip - Day2: observation, record and discussion on the geographical characteristics of target areas
6) No.16-20 Field trip - Day3: observation, record and discussion on the geographical characteristics of target areas
7) No.21-25 Field trip - Day4: observation, record and discussion on the geographical characteristics of target areas
8) No.26-30 Field trip - Day5: observation, record and discussion on the geographical characteristics of target areas

The field trip will be held from 22nd to 26th in September, 2023. The first orientation will be held on the 2nd hour on July 7th.

Detailed schedule will be informed to registered students on this course's manaba page.

履修条件

Students are expected to have taken geography classes (e.g., human and regional geography).
Limited number of students can join the field trip. Instructor's permission are needed to join.

成績評価方法

Assignments in the preliminary research and lectures (20%), participation in the field trip and discussion (40%) ,  presentation of results (10%), final report(30%)

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Study related books, papers, and news papers.
