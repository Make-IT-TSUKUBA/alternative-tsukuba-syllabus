2.0 単位, 2 - 4 年次, 春AB 水5,6
松島 みどり

授業概要

This is an introductory course for students who are eager to improve their English discussion skills. Because this is an introductory course, students are given the opportunity to learn and practice effective delivery of their ideas and attentive listening to others’ opinions as a preparation for discussion. Discussion topics will be related to global issues, and students are expected to actively participate.

授業方法

講義及び演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

1. General-purpose competence: communicative ability, critical and creative thinking skills, broad perspective and internationalism, collaborative, independent & autonomous skills.
2. Special competence: ability of logical expression on international relations, ability of logical expression on international development.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

The objectives of the class are, first, to encourage students to develop their skills in the use of English, and particularly their spoken English, and, second, to develop their powers of analysis and criticism with respect to issues of current concern.

キーワード

授業計画

First three periods (two periods at week1 and 1st period at Week 2) will be conducted through lectures and group discussions.

Then, from 4th period (at third week), session will be conducted at semi-parliamentary style debate. The instructor will introduce the topic and study materials at the beginning, and then students will be divided into 2 groups to read/discuss the study materials with TA and the instructor. Instructor will help to understand the keywords/concepts related to the topic/study materials. At the end of the second period, every student is asked to take any of two resolutions- For and Against.
