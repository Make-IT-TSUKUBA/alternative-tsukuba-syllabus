02AT041   Forum on Sustainable Environmental Studies I [English Class] (持続環境学フォーラムI)

2.0 単位, 1 - 3 年次, 秋AB 月6,7
持続環境学担当教員

授業概要

本フォーラムは,持続循環環境学を枠組みとして,学生の個別研究の学際的な広がりと説明力を育成し,持続可能な物質とエネルギーの循環型社会システムに関する科学技術とその政策的実践力を高度化します.

授業方法

演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

This course is related to the enhancement of students’ capabilities and skills on internationality, communication, teamwork, comprehension/analysis, project management and presentation.
本授業は学生のマネージメント能力、コミュニケーション能力、チームワーク力、国際性、理解力・解析力、課題対応力と提言力の強化を目指す(英語で授業)。

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

(1) To facilitate mutual understanding through discussion among students and professors with different academic background.
(2) To develop global views on environmental protection for a sustainable society.
(3) To better comprehend the skills for communication and presentation.

キーワード

Sustainable society, Environmental standards, Environmental education, Environmental regulation, Green technologies

授業計画

This course is taught in English in the fall semester with intensive lectures. Please pay attention to the notification pasted on the bulletin board outside of the Program Office on October 1. The following is the tentative schedule for this semester.

履修条件

This course is designed for those students who are concerned or interested in environmental issues and countermeasures in developed and developing countries.

成績評価方法

(1) Only those who attended 70% lectures will be evaluated.
(2) The final result of student's performance will be the average value of the professors' evaluation results.
(3) Manaba will be used for student to submit his/her report or presentation and for professor to make comments.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Two days' intensive lectures, group working and final presentation.
