授業概要

This course aims to provide specific knowledge and understanding of the practical implications of law on business transactions in general and on a company in particular. From the perspective of a company engaged in international business transactions, the role and consequences of law for a company’s organization, business model, strategies and day-to-day operation will be explained and illustrated in practical examples and case studies. Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of where, why and how law affects a business and learn how to deal with the legal aspects of various standard business situations.

Course Objectives(Learning Outcomes)

This course aims to provide knowledge and understanding of the practical implications of law on business relations in general and on corporate and commercial issues in particular. From the perspective of a company engaged in international business transactions, the role and consequences of law for a company’s organisation, business model, strategies and day-to-day operation will be explained and illustrated in practical examples and case studies. Students will gain a comprehensive understanding of where, why and how law affects a company’s business and learn how to deal with the legal aspects of various business situations. The course will deal with both civil law and common law concepts and focus on international established legal principles with an emphasis on the legal system in Japan and the EU.

Course Keywords

Class Schedule

The course is covering five general topics, each dealing with a specific aspect of business law. In each class, following a general introduction to the subject and particular topic, case studies for further discussion and analysis in practice will be provided and assigned to groups of students.

1. Corporate Law and Governance
1 Legal principles governing a corporation
2 Stakeholder versus shareholder
3 Management protection and control
4 Corporate governance

2. Merger & Acquisition
1 Legal concepts for growing a business
2 Strategic alliances
3 Starting, running and dissolving a joint venture
4 Takeovers and defense mechanisms

3. Labor Relations
1 General labor law principles
2 Employment protection and its limits
3 Typical labor law issues in a company
4 Voluntary retirement and dismissal  

4. Trade, Commerce and Competition
1 Legal framework governing trade
2 Unfair-competition & anti-trust issues
3 Distributors and sales intermediates

5. Corporate Restructuring and Crisis Management
1 Legal concepts for crisis management
2 Corporate reorganization
3 Corporate rehabilitation
4 Insolvency
