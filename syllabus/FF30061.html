Improve presentation skills related to technical English.

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

It is related to "Communication ability; Broad perspective and international character; Cooperative, independent, and autonomous attitudes; Understanding of physical phenomena; Understanding of chemical phenomena; Understanding of biological phenomena; Mathematical logic and calculation skills".
The undergraduate students normally have difficulty to learn scientific knowledge when they read English textbooks. The course presents the fundamentals of simple, compound, and complex sentences in English paragraphs. There are a lot of exercises promoting those students in college of engineering sciences to be competent in English reading or communication.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

1. The students are capable of using complex sentences to conjugate the main and subordinate clauses.
2. The students are capable of using compound sentences to express equally important information.
3. The students are capable of using simple sentences to exactly operate the full verb, adjective, adverb and prepositional phrases.
4. The students are capable of writing topic sentences to develop one paragraph.

キーワード

simple sentence, compound sentence, complex sentence, topic sentence

授業計画

The fundamental course is presented in items 1~3. Some topics are presented in items 4~8.

第1回 Topic and compound sentences
第2回 Complex sentences
第3回 Simple sentences
第4回 Topic 1: Measurement
第5回 Topics 2: Motion and Vector
第6回 Topic 3: Force Energy and momentum
第7回 Topic 4: Electronics and Magnetics
第8回 Topic 5: Optics

The sentence structures are introduced along online presentation, and then one paper examination is held in one classroom. Topical discussion is presented online. Those fundamentals of English sentences as shown in the items 1~3 are eventually approved in the topical discussion. After the topical discussion, one proposal based on a power-point file is requested.

履修条件

Fundamental study of English in senior and junior high schools.

成績評価方法

Examination (50%), performance (10%), handout (10%), and homework (30%)
The oral and reading responses in the classes are critical to be considered as the performance, which is scored with 10% of the final grade. Handout operation is inspected with 10% of the final grade. Examination is held after the fundamental courses (items 1~3) and scored with 50% of the final grade. The other 30% of the final grade will be considered from the homework.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Lectures (50%), Class exercises (50%).
The handouts should be well operated in or out of the classes. Besides of the exercises for the sentences, the students should read the book chapters, provided from the regular materials.
