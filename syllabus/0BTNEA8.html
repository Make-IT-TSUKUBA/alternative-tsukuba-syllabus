0BTNEA8   感染症と基礎と臨床

1.0 単位, 1・2 年次, 通年 応談
森川 一也

授業概要

感染症基礎医科学：病原体と宿主の相互作用として生じる感染症の基礎医学的学修（細菌学、ウイルス学、寄生虫学、免疫学）。
感染症臨床医学：侵襲性感染症，薬剤耐性菌感染症および施設内感染症に関する疫学調査、病原因子、予防策、治療・対処法を学ぶ。

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Students will be able to explain
1. the biological characteristics of bacteria.
2. the properties of pathogenic and non-pathogenic bacteria.
3. the mechanism and regulation of bacterial pathogenicity.
4. the control of Infectious disease, antimicrobials, and drug resistance.
5. the replication mechanism of parasites & fungi.
6. the molecular pathogenesis of parasites & fungi.
7. the molecular basis of virus genome replication.
8. the molecular pathogenesis of viruses.
9. the outline of virus engineering.
10. the control mechanism & strategy for viruses.
Student will also learn the importance of relevant field including epidemiology, prevention method, diagnosis, treatment etc.

キーワード

Infection, pathogen, parasite, bacteria, virus

授業計画

On-site or on-line lectures. See the manaba.
(2023: Spring semester.)
Student will learn basics on infectious disease through lectures from basic medical aspects (bacteriology, virology, parasitology). In the active learning session, students will select one of the topics and further study infectious diseases from broad aspects including clinical science, epidemiology, pharmacology, etc.
