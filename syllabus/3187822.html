1.0 単位, 1 - 4 年次, 春季休業中 集中
井出 里咲子

授業概要

TOEFL受験対策に特化した集中講座です。コミュニカティブの環境の中で、語彙を増やし、目標スコア(ITP550,iBT79)に到達するためのテクニックを学ぶことによって、本来の実力を確実にスコアにつなげるためのアカデミックスキルを身につけます。

備考

原則としてTOEFL ITP 500-549点の学生対象。履修方法等は後日掲示により周知。卒業予定者は履修不可。
2024年度開講せず。

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

To learn vocablary required for taking the TOEFL test                     
To acquire foundational knowledge of TOEFL test taking                  
To practice taking TOEFL tests

キーワード

Aiming for TOEFL ITP 550

授業計画

5 days × 6 lessons × 50 minutes
     AM: 10:00–12:50(10:00–10:50/11:00–11:50/12:00–12:50)
     PM: 14:00–16:50(14:00–14:50/15:00–15:50/16:00–16:50)
      
●Day 1
Unit 1: Orientation & Introduction to TOEFL - teacher/student introduction, course introduction – Students will be given general information on the TOEFL exam (number of questions and how to prepare for them).
Unit 2: Grammar – Structure ① (understanding sentence structures)
Unit 3: Grammar – Structure ② (structure exercise)
Unit 4: Speaking Basic Skills – Students will learn how to build opinions and express them thoroughly.
Unit 5: Speaking Basic Skills – Students will learn some key phrases to answer each speaking task.
Unit 6: Reading – Passage 1 (paragraph reading and vocabulary exercise)

●Day 2
Unit 7: Writing Basic Skills – Students will learn how to write concise and precise sentences.
Unit 8: Writing Basic skills – Students will learn the basics of academic writing.
Unit 9: Writing – Academic Discussion writing exercise ① (brainstorming)
Unit 10: Writing – Academic Discussion writing exercise ② (essay writing techniques)
Unit 11: Speaking – Task 1 & Task 2 (generating ideas, note-taking exercise, and vocabulary test)
Unit 12: Speaking – Task 2 &Task 3 (organizing information)

●Day 3
Unit 13: Listening Basic Skills – Students will learn phonics rules, practice various sounds, and identify the differences.
Unit 14: Listening Basic Skills – Students will learn efficient note-taking skills to understand conversations and lectures.
Unit 15: Listening – Part A (dialogue listening techniques, idioms)
Unit 16: Listening – Part B (dialogue listening techniques and dictation exercise)
Unit 17: Writing – Integrated writing exercise ① (reading/listening discussion and vocabulary test)
Unit 18: Writing – Integrated writing exercise ② (writing logical paragraphs)

●Day 4
Unit 19: Speaking – Task 3 &Task 4 (discussion and summarizing strategies)
Unit 20: Speaking – Integrated practice (time management)
Unit 21: Listening – Part B & Part C (lecture listening techniques and vocabulary test)

Unit 22: Reading – Passage 2 (reading techniques)
Unit 23: Reading – Passage 3 (reading comprehension strategy 1)
Unit 24: Reading – Passage 4 (reading comprehension strategy 2)

●Day 5
Unit 25: Grammar – Written expression ① (understanding written expressions and vocabulary test)
Unit 26: Grammar – Written expression ② (written expression exercise)
Unit 27: Listening – Part C (comprehending academic lectures)
      PM:  TOEFL  ITP Test
      
    ※The contents may be changed depending on the level and understanding of the students.

成績評価方法

  Writing tasks: 35%, two writing tasks are graded by a rubric.
  Speaking tasks: 35%, two speaking tasks are graded by a rubric.
  Participation: 20%, classroom participation is graded by a rubric.
  Vocabulary test: 10%, four vocabulary tests are graded by a rubric.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Students can prepare for speaking tasks, writing tasks, and vocabulary tests at home by reviewing the course material.
Teachers may ask students to read passages before class. Please always follow the instructions.
