0AFM313   Technology Management

1.0 単位, 1・2 年次, 秋B 水7,8
平井 孝志

授業概要

この講義では, ディスカッション, ケーススタディ形式も組み合わせ, 技術経営(マネジメント・オブ・テクノロジー)の全体像を把握・理解することを目的に開講される. それは言い換えると「企業がどのように価値を創造をおこない, そこから生じる価値をどのように獲得していくべきか」という課題に対して, 実務上の示唆を得ることを目的としているとも解釈できる. この講義では, 技術経営とイノベーションに関連するコア・コンペテンスや競争戦略に関するトピックについても触れ, 技術経営と戦略との接点を探っていく.

Course Objectives(Learning Outcomes)

In this course, we will examine wide varieties of topic related with technology and innovation, such as R&D management, s-curve and de-maturity, disruptive innovation, product architecture, core technology strategy, core rigidity, and ambidexterity, and Business Ecosystem. Through multidisciplinary discussions, we will deepen students’ understanding of MOT.
We also use several business cases of Japanese corporations for class discussions. Students are expected to read case assignments and distributed papers be- fore/after the class and participate actively in class.

Course Keywords

Value creation, Value capture, Core technology strategy, Business architecture, Ecosystem

Class Schedule

Class Session 1: What is MOT? Why is it important? : We will start to discuss the definition of innovation. With understanding the important issues on innovation, we will clarify the basic framework of MOT, which consists of value creation and value capture. Preparation: Read Handout posted on Manaba and prepare one or two page memo on a following question in order for case discussion (please bring your own copy for a group discussion);‒ Sharp used to be the leader in the LCD market, but its business performance has declined dramatically since 2008. Please examine the factors that contributed to the company’s decline.
Class Session 2: Value Creation and R&D management: Using a video titled “The Century of Innovation: American History #4, Transitorized!, From vacuum tubes to transistors ̃The birth and development of the semiconductor industry ̃”, we will examine value creation and R&D management.
Class Session 3: Core Technology Strategy and Ecosystem: We will examine the characteristics of core technology strategy. We also examine the innovation processes and business ecosystem and how to overcome discontinuity of technology evolution.
Class Session 4: Architecture and Value Capture: We will review product/business architecture and examine important factors for transformation and profit formula development. We will conclude this course by examining desirable MOT leadership.
Class Session 5: Group Presentation
