授業概要

社会経済事象に関わる国際⽐較等について講義をおこなう。いくつかの国際データセットを⽤いながら、⽣活⽔準や経済成⻑率などを例に、国際間⽐較を⾏う視点やその相違の背景について学ぶ。次に、それらの事象を説明する経済モデルについて紹介し、実際のデータを⽤いて計量的に同定するために必要となる基礎的な理論について学ぶ。

Students are able to specify and estimate their own econometric models with economic data.

Applied econometric analysis; Regression models.

授業計画

The course is about applied econometric analysis with application to international economic comparisons. The course will proceed in the following four steps.

At the beginning we will use several international datasets, and examine the wide range of living standards across countries, and how they can be explained empirically.
Next I will introduce the basic approach for applied econometric analysis, focusing on the following topics: (a) how to specify an econometric model, and interpret results of its estimation, (b) how to evaluate regression models, and choose the most preferred one, (c) how to avoid most common problems in applied econometric analysis.
Using several published papers that studied international differences in living standards and in economic growth, students will study how applied econometric analysis was used in practice, and replicate published results.
Finally, I will discuss recent advances in machine learning (a subfield of AI) that are related conventional econometric models, and introduce several popular machine learning approaches to regression analysis (regression trees, bagging, random forests, boosting, and automatic machine learning (AutoML)). Students will have chance to compete with computer in creating a superior empirical model to analyse international differences in living standards.

Econometric analysis will be introduced using Excel, with optional discussion of other software (R, Eviews, etc.) if requested by students.
