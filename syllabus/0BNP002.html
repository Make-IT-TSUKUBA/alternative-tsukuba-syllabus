授業概要

環境問題の現場で集中授業を行う。種々のステークホルダーとのワークショップ等を通じて、幅広い視野・考察力・倫理観を涵養する。ワークショップの企画・運営を担うことにより、実践的なマネジメント力と問題解決能力等を身につける。

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

1. Learn from Minamata. You will get a profound impact from Minamata disease and start considering how the environmental problems are complicated. For this purpose, you will investigate the histories and science of Minamata by group works disease before the trip.
2. Make good colleagues. Because Ph D course is tough, you need friends to help and encourage each other. By sharing this class, you will gain friends who will help you in the Ph D course and your long career.
3. Learn management. After getting Ph D, sooner or later you will be a PI (principal investigator) who needs Management skills of handling an international community. Each member should take leaderships in management of the group works.

授業計画

This is an on-line forum. We will go on a virtual field trip of three days in Minamata City, which is well-known for Minamata Disease caused by severe water pollution starting from 1950's. We will visit the hot-spots of the pollution, see the recovery process, and see the victims of the disease.
All the programs will be delivered in English.
The following schedule is a tentative one. Depending on the situation (especially COVID-19), it may change.

2022-10-07 Fri. 16:00-17:30 Forum 2 Online orientation

2022-10-25 Tue. 14:30-17:30 Forum 2 seminar 1 (group presentation)
Topic 1: Outline and history of the Minamata pollution & disease
Topic 2: Biology and chemistry of the Minamata disease.
Topic 3: Local culture, industry, and economy in Minamata before and after the Chisso factory.

2022-11-01 Tue. 14:30-17:30 Forum 2 seminar 2 (group presentation)
Topic 4: Fight of Minamata disease by the victims against Chsso company, Kumamoto Prefecture, and Japanese Government.
Topic 5: Restoration of pollution
Topic 6: Impacts of Minamata disease in environmental science, engineering, and policies in the world.
