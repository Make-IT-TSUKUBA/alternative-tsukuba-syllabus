BE21151   Readings of Japanese Studies

2.0 単位, 2 年次, 秋B 集中

授業概要

This course provides an introduction to modern and contemporary Japanese history and politics.  It has three main focuses: 1) the formation of symbol emperor system,  2)Failed democratization in Prewar, 3) hereditary candidates in political parties.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

To attain basic understanding of Japanese history and politics
Lectures, assigned readings, and discussion in class provide the intellectual architecture for students to create their own understandings of comtemporal political and social processes in Japan.

キーワード

democratization, hereditary candidates, symbol emperor system
