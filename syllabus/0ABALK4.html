授業概要

This course provides a structured approach to integrating data science with English language teaching. It starts with basic data science concepts and progresses to specialized areas relevant to language education. Practical training in R equips students with skills in data analysis and visualization. The curriculum includes an in-depth look at generative AI's application in language teaching across several sessions. The course also focuses on adapting teaching methods based on data insights and addresses ethical aspects of using AI in education. Theoretical learning is applied through two projects: one in data analysis and another in designing a technology-based teaching strategy. This balance of theory and practice prepares students to apply data science effectively in language education.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

The course aims to equip students with data science skills applicable to English language teaching. It focuses on essential techniques such as data summarization, statistical methods, machine learning, and text mining, along with hands-on training in R for data analysis and visualization. Central to the course is the exploration of generative AI in language education, covered in three detailed sessions. Additionally, the course emphasizes adaptivity in language teaching, informed by data science. Ethical considerations of AI in educational contexts are also examined. The course concludes with two major projects: one on practical data analysis and another on creating a technology-enhanced teaching method, integrating theoretical knowledge with practical skills.
