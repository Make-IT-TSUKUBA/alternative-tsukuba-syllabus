0ATQ215   Elite Sport Coaching (エリートスポーツコーチング論)

1.0 単位, 2 年次, 秋AB 集中
仙石 泰雄

授業概要

一流競技者のパフォーマンス向上に関するコーチング、トレーニング理論及び方法を学び実践する。特に、高強度トレーニング、ファンクショナルトレーニングの処方に焦点をあて、様々な最新トレーニング用具を活用する方法について学習し、特定の運動種目を対象としたトレーニングプログラムを作成する。

備考

英語で授業。
対面
GSI302

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

Ability to use knowledge、Understanding of Olympic/Paralympic Studies、Specialized understanding relating to physical education/sport/health、Ability to develop Sport and Olympic Studies.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Students will be engaged in achieving practical coaching ability to enhance performance in elite athlete by learning the latest coaching and training theory and discussing the actual coaching situation in the world.

キーワード

High Intensity Interval Training, Functional Training, Conditioning

授業計画

The type of the course will face to face.
Submit the final report to the Manaba course page.
Include all of the following information in your training program.
(1) The sports event, performance level, age and gender of the
Participants
(2) All the training program (HIIT, Functional Training and Conditioning)
you learned at the class
Your training program should integrate materials and information you learned at the class. The training program should be prepared considering that anyone can do your training by reading your program. So it is recommended to include illustration or pictures in your training program.

1. Guidance, Coaching System in Japan

3. High Intensity Interval Training: Practice
4. Functional Training with suspension trainer : Theory
5. Functional Training with suspension trainer : Practice
6. Functional Training with tubing device : Theory
7. Functional Training with tubing device : Practice
8. Water Exercise Program: Theory
9. Water Exercise Program: Practice
10. Prescription of a specific training program

履修条件

成績評価方法

This course is assessed by coursework (including presentation and discussion in lectures) and final report.
The weighting is 30% coursework, 70% final report.
Taking attendance directly at the class.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Each credit requires 45 hours of study time, including class time (75 minutes x number of classes) . After each class, students should prescribe a exercise related to your own specific sport event, and prepare the training program for the final report.
