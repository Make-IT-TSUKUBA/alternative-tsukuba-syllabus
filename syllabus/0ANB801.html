授業概要

地縁技術と先端技術を結ぶインターフェースとして必要な基盤的バイオテクノロジーに関する知識の習得を目的とする.植物,食品加工などに関連したバイオテクノロジーの話題を各分野の専門家が解説する.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

This class introduces both old and modern Plant biotechnology technologies that contribute to human life and society. The main objective of this class is to obtain the capability to scientifically discuss those technologies. This class also learns about current plant molecular biotechnologies that are useful for human life. We also learn about history of genetic engineering and genetically modified organisms (GMOs) as well as the regulatory assessment and issues for GMOs.

キーワード

Genetic modified organisms, genome editing technologies, plant metabolites, tissue culture methods, effective microorganisms, plant volatiles, plant herbicides

授業計画

In principle, this lecture will be taken place in person.
However, we may hold classes by online depending on the availability of the instructor.
When online lectures are to be held, we will notify the students via manaba one week prior to the lecture.
Please note that the 6th week class by Prof Yamada maybe held by online.
This course introduces the fundamentals of plant biotechnology and research trends in its application, aiming at the acquisition of basic knowledge and applied skills.

第1回 Introduction and guidance
第2回 Genome editing technologies and next generation sequencing technologies
第3回 Biotechnology in Plant Breeding
第4回 Practical Plant Biotechnology
第5回 Utilization of biotechnology on fruit production.
第6回 Determination and visualization of small molecules in plant tissue
第7回 Useful metabolites production using plant cell cultures
第8回 Metabolic engineering in plants
第9回 Biotechnology in Food Production
第10回 Microbiology for plant-biomass refinery technology

履修条件

成績評価方法

Students must attend at least six of the ten classes to be eligible for a grade; attendance of less than five or fewer will not be graded. Grades will be determined by report scores; students will be given report assignments by nine professors, and will need to submit by the specified date. Your grade will be determined by the average of your scores.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

関連分野の成書や論文の熟読。発表資料とレポートの作成。
Read the books and articles in related fields. Preparation of presentation materials and reports.
