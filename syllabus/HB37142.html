メイヤーズ トーマス

授業概要

This course is designed to increase students’ confidence and ability to discuss, debate, and make presentations in English on a range on current topics and issues in medicine and medical research. The aim of the course is to develop students’ international understanding and professionalism towards becoming a member of the global medical community.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

The purpose of this class is to improve students' critical thinking by discussing the latest medical topics and research in English, with an emphasis on fostering a deeper international understanding and a professional outlook. Students can learn medical and research related vocabulary, useful professional English, and comprehend recent medicine-related news.
