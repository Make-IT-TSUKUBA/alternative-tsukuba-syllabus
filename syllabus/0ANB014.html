授業概要

“Culture is to humans as water is to fish”. Even though we cannot
function outside a culture, culture has intangible aspects preventing us
from realizing what our own medium is. This course will take us on a
journey to elevate many of those unspoken norms and basic assumptions of
own and other culture into consciousness.
A fascinating aspect of crossing cultures is the unique way it can
become informing and open up access to creative problem solving. This
can lead to personal and organizational growth. Nevertheless,
communication between individuals from different cultures can also lead
to a spectrum of negative reactions, from slight emotional discomfort
all to way to conflict.
The course will build theoretic knowledge, it is highly interactive,
experiential and reflection-based.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

This course aims to provide three main tools, necessary for effective intercultural communication:
• self- and other- culture awareness;
• theoretical knowledge (focus on cultural dimensions and communication styles);
• skills and attitudes to communicate effectively across cultures

キーワード

Cultural dimension, Intercultural communication, Cross-cultural competence, Active Listening; Gene-culture
