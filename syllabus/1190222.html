授業概要

This course is designed to help students become familiar with the educational system and campus life at the University of Tsukuba. Important information regarding living in Japan as a foreign resident and a general introduction on living in Tsukuba city will also be provided.Lecture is conducted in English. face-to face.

備考

For students in Interdisciplinary Program in Life and Environmental Sciences
英語で授業。

科目群

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

The objective of this course is to help English Program Freshman to adjust to living in Japan and studying in the university.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

Students will be introduced to the various systems of the university including TWINS, Manaba, etc. They will learn how they can beg help, when necessary, and live a life at the university, which include a lecture on Mental Health.

キーワード

Life-in-Japan, Campus-life, course registration

授業計画

Details will be announced in the first class of the course.

第1回 Introduction of Life in Tsukuba and the University of Tsukuba
第2回 Orientation of curriculum registration
第3回 Tour of Library
第4回 Tour of a Scientific Laboratory
第5回 Mental Health
第6回 LES (School of Life and Environmental Sciences) orientation
第7回 Guidance for safety life in Tsukuba

All the classes will be held face-to-face.
The order of lectures is subject to change.
