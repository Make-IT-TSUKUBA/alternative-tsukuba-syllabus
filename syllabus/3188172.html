授業の到達目標(学修成果)

Upon Completion of this Course, you will:
Have honed your critical reading skills through the analysis of reading materials and essays written by your peers.
Have emerged from the course with the ability to effectively apply your English skills to academic writing. This includes crafting well-structured essays, synthesizing information from assigned readings, and expressing complex ideas coherently.
Have enhanced your ability to contribute thoughtfully to conversations, fostering a collaborative and intellectually stimulating learning environment.

キーワード

critical thinking, research skills, essay writing skills, peer-review, referencing and citation skills

授業計画

This course is tailored for university students aiming to apply their acquired English skills to academic writing. It presupposes that students have reached a proficiency level where English serves as an intellectual tool. The course explores topics that encourage critical thinking and active engagement.
Structured around essay reading and writing exercises, class sessions also emphasize speaking and listening activities, fostering a dynamic exchange of ideas. Students are expected to come prepared having completed assigned readings/viewings, ready to contribute to class discussions. While the course is reading-intensive, the materials are selected for enjoyment and interest. The goal of this course is to cultivate a vibrant classroom environment where collective thinking about the subjects studied can thrive. Success in this course hinges on sustained effort and self-discipline. Consistent, pre-lesson study sessions are recommended to maintain a steady and comfortable learning pace.
