1.0 単位, 1 年次, 秋学期 応談

授業概要

In this course students will learn more advanced practical reading strategies within the framework of "English for general academic purposes". In addition to deepening understanding of the English language, students can improve skills in critical thinking, analysis and power of expression.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

This course aims to help students to sharpen their conventional reading comprehension skills as well as encourage them to explore and apply a handful of practical strategies that reinforce their critical reading comprehension. Students will learn how to use prior knowledge, experiences, careful thought and evaluation and apply them to all reading situations.  The course also includes a bonus session on speed-reading.

キーワード

Reading comprehension, critical reading strategies, Summarizing, Questioning, Pre-reading and Predicting, Speed Reading

授業計画

"Week 1: 7 Principles of Active and Efficient Reading. Short Pre-   test.
Week 2: Getting the Essential Information (“Summarizing” / GIST technique)
Week 3: Finding the Main Idea (+ “Prior Knowledge” Technique)
Week 4: Defining Vocabulary in Context (+ “Vocabulary Knowledge” Technique)  
Week 5: Fact vs. Opinion (+ “Think aloud” Technique)
Week 6: Combining the skills (+ “Self-Questioning” strategies)
Week 7: Working through passages (+ “Prediction and Inference” strategies)  
Week 8: Order of Importance (+ “Semantic mapping” strategies)
Week 9: Using comparisons (+“Mental imaging” strategies)
Week 10: Assuming Causes and Predicting Effects (+“Text features” Strategies)
Week 11: BONUS: Introduction to Speed-reading (part 1)
Week 12: BONUS: Introduction to Speed-reading (part 2)
Week 13: Review of Strategies. Drills.
Week 14: Review of Strategies. Drills.
Week 15: Review of Strategies. Final Reading Test.
