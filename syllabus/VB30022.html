授業概要

These seminars will approach a wide range of academic knowledge, evaluation methods, and coping and preventive strategies related to the issues of social diversity-inclusion as well as human health-wellbeing.
For the former part, you will learn a variety of global issues concerning the disciplines of humanities and social sciences.  You will learn what are problems and discuss how we can resolve them.
    As for the latter part, the course will approach the health matters of aged people, as contemporary society requires information literacy and care of the elderly. You will learn basic concepts and techniques of a survey to grasp problems in a real situation, processing methods, and interpretative strategies for gathered information (documents, data). In the end, you will come up with an action plan and/or original research project aiming at finding and solving problems of a real situation.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

[Health & Wellness Issues]   
-learn basic knowledge on public health approaches to promote health behaviors (mainly physical activity promotion and sedentary behavior reduction) through the application of behavioral epidemiology.
-learn the basic concepts of the behavioral science theories and models utilized in approaches to promote health behaviors, and know about the various practices that apply them.
-learn advanced techniques of a research on public health field (including relevant research article searching and reviewing, measurements selection, data collecting and analyzing methods, and interpretative strategies for obtaining results).

[Social Issues]   
-gain a better understanding of global social problems related to SDGs from critical perspectives.
-discuss possible solutions for these social issues recognizing the difficulties to implement them.

成績評価方法

This course is evaluated by the mixture of both health & wellness issues and social issues. The evaluation in each issue is converted into 100 points and the final evaluation is made on the average of the two scores.

[Health & Wellness Issues]
Focusing on the three items listed in the attainment objectives, grading is based on achievement through 1) proactivity by discission in class, 2) response sheets (summary of key points and feedback), 3) presentations, and 3) reports.

1) Proactivity by discission in class (30%)
2) Response sheet (10%)
3) Presentations (30%)

* The class proactivity and response sheets in each session are scored at 10 points each, and the average score is then calculated. Each presentation is also scored at 10 points each, and then the average scores for the report and presentation are calculated. Finally, the three evaluation items are scored by proportion of the four evaluation items.

[Social Issues]   
Weekly assignment on reflection of the class and further research on the topic: 100%
- Some classes may require students to present, in such a case, presentation will be the basis of the evaluation.
