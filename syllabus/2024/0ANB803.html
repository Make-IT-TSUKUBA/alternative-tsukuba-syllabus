0ANB803   Introduction to Sustainable Agriculture in Rural Areas

2.0 単位, 1・2 年次, 秋AB 水3,4
トファエル アハメド

授業概要

This course provides concepts and practices of sustainable agriculture in rural areas covering soil fertility, chemical application systems, site-specific management systems from pre-harvest to post-harvest stages in production. Furthermore, the participatory rural appraisal for sustainable agricultural practices, supply and value chain analysis in agribusiness, ICT, IoT, and Big Data topics are included in discussion and course project.

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

This course is designed to provide basic knowledge and learning of sustainable agriculture and practices in rural areas. Graduate students from multidisciplinary areas will acquire fundamental knowledges of agricultural practices to reduce chemicals in agricultural production.

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

• To develop and use reasoning skill for analyzing sustainable agricultural systems in rural areas
• To acquire and use selected techniques to develop sustainable agricultural practices
• To become acquainted with all types of problems, measure and means to develop sustainable agricultural practices

キーワード

Soil Structure and Fertility, Sustainable Water Management at Farm, Management of Pests  and Chemical Application Systems, Site-specific Management, Participatory Approaches, Decision Support Systems

授業計画

An in depth discussion of "state-of-the-art" practices is presented in this course. The catalog description highlights: Soil structure and fertility, sustainable water management at farm, management of pests and chemical application systems, site-specific management, participatory approaches, sustainable agribusiness and ICT planning for sustainable agriculture in rural areas, food security concepts and discussion.
