授業概要

このコースでは, 管理職が経済学基礎理論とグローバル市場を理解するために必要とされる, ミクロ経済学・マクロ経済学の基本的な概念に焦点を当てる. 需要・供給と価格決定のメカニズム, 消費者の選択理論(効用概念, 限界効用, 予算制約, 代替効果, 所得効果, 価格弾力性, 上級財, 下級財), 企業の利潤最大化のメカニズム(収入, 費用, 限界利益, 損益分岐点, 操業停止点), ケインズモデル(総支出モデル, 投資乗数, 政府支出乗数), 景気循環と失業・インフレのメカニズムの理解を目標とし, 講義とクラス演習を交えながら学修する.

Course Objectives(Learning Outcomes)

This course focuses on basic concepts in Economics, which are needed for managers to understand the theory of economics and global market. It shows students how to evaluate demand and supply, how to determine the Keynesian aggregate demand, and how to identify business cycle.

Course Keywords

Day1 :Demand curve, Supply curve, Market Equilibrium, Consumer surplus, Producer surplus, Demand elasticity
Day 2:Utility theory, Opportunity set, Budget constrain, Giffen goods, Consumer’s demand curve, Normal goods, Inferior goods
Day3:Economic profit, Accounting profit, Economic rent, Total revenue, Average revenue, Marginal revenue, Average total cost, Average variable cost, Marginal cost, Profit maximization, Shutdown point, Day4:Perfectly competition, Monopolistic competition, Oligopoly, Monopoly
Day5:Monetary policy, Fiscal policy, Mix of Fiscal and Monetary policy, Quantitative easing

Class Schedule

Day1 :(Micro 1) Demand & Supply   (Chapter 1) Ono
 Explain the principles of demand and supply
 Calculate and interpret the amount of excess demand or excess supply associated with a nonequilibrium price.
 Calculate and interpret consumer surplus, producer surplus, and total surplus.
 Analyze the effect of government regulation and intervention on demand and supply
Day 2:(Micro 2) Demand & Supply (Consumer Demand) (Chapter 2) Ono
 Describe the use of indifference curves, opportunity sets, and budget constraints in decision making.
 Determine a consumer’s equilibrium bundle of goods based on utility analysis.
 Distinguish between normal goods and inferior goods, and explain Giffen goods and Veblen goods in this context.
Day3:(Micro 3) Demand & Supply (The firm) (Chapter 3) Ono
 Calculate, interpret, and compare accounting profit, economic profit, normal profit, and economic rent.
 Calculate and interpret total, average, marginal, fixed, and variable costs.
 Determine and describe breakdown and shutdown points of production.
Day4:(Micro 1) The Firm and Market Structure (Chapter 4) Ono
 Describe the characteristics of perfect competition, monopolistic competition, oligopoly, and pure monopoly.
 Explain the relationships among price, marginal revenue, marginal cost, economic profit, and the elasticity of demand under each market structure.
 Describe and determine the optimal price and output for firms under each market structure.
Day5:(Macro 1) Monetary and Fiscal Policy (Chapter 7) Ono
 Compare monetary policy and fiscal policy.
 Describe theories of the demand for and supply of money.
 Describe the roles and objectives of central banks.
 Determine whether a monetary policy is expansionary or contractionary.
 Describe the roles and objectives of fiscal policy.
 Determine whether a fiscal policy is expansionary or contractionary.
