0BNP001   環境学フォーラムI

2.0 単位, 1 - 3 年次, 秋AB 集中
環境学担当教員

授業概要

本フォーラムは,持続循環環境学を枠組みとして,学生の個別研究の学際的な広がりと説明力を育成し,持続可能な物質とエネルギーの循環型社会システムに関する科学技術とその政策的実践力を高度化します.

備考

英語で授業。
対面(オンライン併用型)
詳細はmanabaの掲示板にて周知予定。英語で授業。対面授業(オンライン併用型)で実施する。

授業方法

演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

This course is related to the enhancement of students’ capabilities and skills on internationality, communication, teamwork, comprehension/analysis, project management and presentation.
本授業は学生のマネージメント能力、コミュニケーション能力、チームワーク力、国際性、理解力・解析力、課題対応力と提言力の強化を目指す(英語で授業)。

Generic Competences; Knowledge creation competence, Management competence, Leadership competence, Internationality competence. Specific Competences; Specialized comprehension ability / analysis ability, Application ability, Suggestion ability, Ability for problem-solving.
汎用コンピテンス; 知の創成力、マネージメント能力、リーダーシップ力、国際性. 専門コンピテンス; 理解力・解析力、応用力、提言力、問題解決力.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

(1) To facilitate mutual understanding through discussions among students and professors with different academic backgrounds.
(2) To develop global views on environmental issues and countermeasures for a sustainable society.
(3) To better comprehend the skills for communication and presentation.

キーワード

sustainable development, Developed and developing countries, Environmental issues, Countermeasures, Regulations

授業計画

This course is taught in English in the fall semester with intensive invited lectures from developed and developing countries. The detailed date and schedule will be notified at the beginning of October via manaba.

第1回 Online orientation at the beginning of October via online system
第2回 9:00-18:00, Intensive Lectures and Theme Discussion. Venue: Natural Science Building C501 and online system
第3回 9:00-18:00, Intensive Lectures and Theme Discussion.  Venue: Natural Science Building C501 and online system
第4回 9:00-18:00, Group Working I and Outline Presentation. Venue: Natural Science Building C501 and online system
第5回 9:00-18:00, Group Working II and Final Presentation. Venue: Natural Science Building C501 and online system

Will notify all the students through manaba if there's any change. Besides the onsite venue, we'll also use online meetings to conduct the invited lectures and reports.

履修条件

The students are welcome, who are concerned or interested in environmental issues and countermeasures in developed and developing countries.

成績評価方法

(1) Only those who attended 70% lectures will be evaluated.
(2) The final result of a student's performance will be the average value of the professors' evaluation results.
(3) Manaba will be used for student to submit his/her report or presentation and for professor to make comments.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Reading related publications and online documents used in the lectures can improve students' understanding of the related topics, and help them deliver a logic, understandable, and well-organized presentation.
