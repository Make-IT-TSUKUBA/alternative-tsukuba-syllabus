02DMP15   政治とインターネット3A

1.0 単位, 1 - 3 年次, 春AB 応談
川﨑 レスリー

授業概要

The main purpose of this course is to provide students with an overview of theories regarding the use of the Internet for political purposes, including political campaigns as well as advocacy and social movements. We will be focusing mainly on theoretical uses of the Internet supplemented by case studies in certain national milieu and for designated social topics.

備考

英語で授業。
対面(オンライン併用型)

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

Generic Competences: Capacity for Creative Scientific Research, Communication Ability, Global Awareness
Specific Competences: Research Ability, Expertise Acquisition

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

1. Capacity for creative scientific research: Acquire advanced theoretical knowledge of campaigns, advocacy, and social movements
2. Communication ability: Individual written-oral output will contribute to enhancing students’ communicative abilities.
3. Global awareness: Acquire knowledge of the use of the Internet for political campaign purposes in an internationally comparable perspective.
4. Research ability: In-depth research tasks (reading journal papers and other academic articles.
5. Expertise acquisition: Practical aspect expertise can be gained through this course.

キーワード

Politics, Advocacy, Internet and Politics, Communication

授業計画

This course is structured as a combination of lectures, discussion, and student presentations.
Students are required to do the appropriate readings and review for each class, as well as come to class prepared for discussion. The reading load for
this course will give students the opportunity to sharpen their skills in managing academic resources and their time.
Please note that the course schedule and content may be changed due to the number of students taking the course and student progress in the course
materials.

履修条件

The course “Global Society and Information 2” (0BBE702 / 02DMP16 ), held in the Fall AB semester is related to this class. Students should be prepared to continue deeper in the themes explored in the earlier class. In addition, students who take this course must have sufficient understanding and grasp of English and Japanese to be able to work with theoretical resource materials.

成績評価方法

Active participation in discussing readings and other activities in the course: 20%
Two writing assignments designed to check reflect student comprehension of the lecture materials and readings: 20% each (total 40%)
Individual presentation and report: 40%
Total: 100%

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

Students should be prepared to spend a significant amount of time doing the readings each week and preparing for class discussions.
