授業概要

This course provides a historical overview on the management style and labor relations from comparisons of social institutions and corporate practices. By taking examples from Japan, Europe and other countries, we would examine how people work and discuss their merits and demerits, their possibilities and challenges.

講義

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

This course explores the convergent and divergent development of employment relations in the globalization, liberalization and digitization. We would pick up several conceptual framework and the mechanism of global production chain along with the national institutional dimensions like the US., Germany, Japan and other countries.

1. General-propose competence: critical and creative thinking skills, broad perspective and internationalism.
2. Special competence: understanding international development, analytical skills on international development.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Based on the text book, Comparative Employment Relations in the Global Economy,  this course explores the convergent and divergent development of employment relations in the globalization, liberalization and digitization. Through the presentations from the students on each chapter, we would first pick up several conceptual framework and the mechanism of global production chain. Then the students will pick up a country, and have presentations on each national employment relations like the US., Germany, Japan and other countries, based on the textbook or on their own research. .
Students will be able to have more insights in the relations of employment and economy from both the comparative and global point of view. Through the opportunities of their own presentations and researches, students could also develop their presentation skills and analytical skills on the topics they choose.

キーワード

comparative employment relations, global production chain, labor relations, labor unions, precarious work, contingent work, gig work

授業計画

Students choose the topic  from the textbook on which they would like to present. At the first class we will explain the textbook and the students would be divided into several groups. For five classes, they will have presentation of PPT on the theory and conceptual frameworks, For the latter four classes, they will have presentations on the research on national employment relations of each country, depending on the textbook of on their own study.

第1回 We'll explain the textbook and the students would be divided into several groups.
第2回 Week 2-6. Reading textbooks and learning the basic concepts of the comparative employment relations through the students' presentations, explanations, and discussions.
第3回 Week 7-10. Presentations of students on the national employment relations on each country on which the student is interested.

Some individuals could make their own presentation, apart from the textbooks or articles. They could select their own theme, which would be included in the main research targets of this class.
