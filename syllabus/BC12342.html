BC12342   開発と金融

2.0 単位, 2 - 4 年次, 春AB 木3,4
鈴木 英明

授業概要

This course will discuss what modality of finance will be needed for achieving 2030 Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), based on the understanding that conventional type of development finance such as ODAs will be vastly inadequate for SDGs. The discussion will take up a variety of new financing modalities for development, taking into account ongoing works at global forum such as the World Bank. This course will be conducted in English. Students need to expect heavy workload  in terns of reading requirement and class presentation.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Students are expected to gain the ability to understand and analyze a variety of financing modalities available for economic development.

キーワード

Sustainable development goals, economic development, ODA, tax system, expenditure reform, PPP, private investment, international financial institutions.

授業計画

Week 1-2: Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and domestic resource mobilization
Week 3: Student presentation
Week 4: Public expenditure reform and ODA
Week 5: Student presentation
Week 6: Private finance (investment climate, microfinace, etc.)
Week 7: Student presentation
Week 8: Public Private Partnership, Innovative finance, social investments, etc.
Week 9: Student presentation
Week 10: New role of international financial institutions

履修条件

Prerequisite: "International Economics" (BC51071) offered by International Studies Department or its equivalent. Those students who have not taken "International Economics" but wish to register this course need to consult the instructor.

成績評価方法

3)  Four class questions (3 points each, 12 points)
4)  Final quiz (20 points)
