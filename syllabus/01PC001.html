授業概要

ビジネスの成功は, 人々に依るところが大きい. このコースは, 個人がどのように職場で行動するか, どのように動機付けられ, どのように相互作用するかについて, 基本的な知識を学生に提供するように設計されている. より具体的には, コース内容は個人, グループ, 組織の3つの次元に分類される. それぞれの次元について, 理論と実践の融合について考察する. クラスは, 講義, ディスカッション, ケーススタディとロールプレイにより構成される.

講義

Teaching methods

The success of business depends to a large extent on its people at all organizational levels. This course is designed to provide students with fundamental knowledge about how individuals behave in the workplace, how they are motivated and how they interact with each other. Specifically, course content comprises the three dimensions of individual, group and organizational behavior. For each dimension, integration between theory and implication will be considered. Classes will be a mixture of lectures, discussions and case studies.

Types of competencies expected to be gained after taking this course

Problem finding: Accept change, anticipate problems
Decision making; Analytical orientation, creative thinking
Implication: Organizational management, communication

Course Objectives(Learning Outcomes)

This course is designed to develop students’ fundamental knowledge and analytical skills regarding managerial behavior in organizations from a multidisciplinary point of view. After taking this course, students will study the issues and complexities of making decisions related to interpersonal, group and organizational levels of organizational management and behavior. Cross-cultural dimensions of organizational behavior will be also discussed in recognition of the increasingly global nature of industry and society as a whole.

Course Keywords

1. Organizational Management and Behavior
2. Individual Behavior: Attitudes, Job Satisfaction; Motivation, Decision-Making
3. Group Behavior: Groups and Teams, Leadership and Power Politics
4. Organizational Behavior: Organizational Culture and Change Management
5. Cross Cultural Understanding
