授業概要

This course familiarizes students with corpus tools and corpus analysis as a hands-on training. In so doing, each student makes a summary presentation in English on given topics.

備考

2018年度以前入学の人文学類生を対象とする。
オンライン(同時双方向型)
The classroom language is English.

授業方法

演習

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

Related to "2. Critical & creative thinking" and "4. Understanding of language"

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

1) The students obtain hands-on knowledge and experiences of conducting a corpus analysis with corpus tools.
2) The students learn to make a presentation on given topics in English with PowerPoint slides.

キーワード

PowerPoint_presentation, corpora, BYU, concordancer, query, tagging

授業計画

1) Lectures are given in English.
2) Students’ presentations are also done in English with PowerPoint slides.
3) The contents and their order are subject to change.
4) Online real-time interactive learning is planned. Refer to the manaba handout/notice for more details.

第1回 Introduction to Online Corpora
第2回 Use of Corpus of Contemporary American English (COCA)
第3回 Some features of American English
第4回 Use of British National Corpus (BNC)
第5回 Some features of British English
第6回 Use of TIME Magazine Corpus
第7回 Analysis with a chronological corpus
第8回 Use of Corpus of American Soap Operas
第9回 Analysis of spoken language
第10回 Summary and explanation of the exam
