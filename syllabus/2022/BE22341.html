授業の到達目標（学修成果）

The objective to learn the legal system will be more or less "becoming a lawyer."  Lawyers generally apply basic concepts to the real cases.  Law does not work as an idea.  It will absolutely be made best use by solving the real cases.  This is true in the area of commercial law, especially corporate law.  Japanese corporate law is somewhat strange because it has roots in the European system but is grafted by the American system.  That's why we can compare the both concepts on each theme.  That makes us to derive very rational answer to solve the problems. So in this class, first I will give you a couple of basic legal concepts of corporate law in Japan.  And then, let's try to solve the real cases, fictitious cases, or cases on Japanese Bar Examinations.  After finishing this class you may be "petit lawyer" or "quasi lawyer."
