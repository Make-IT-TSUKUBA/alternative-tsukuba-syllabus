VB20023   Practical Training on Global Issues A-II

3.0 単位, 3 年次, 春ABC 木5,6
森尾 貴広, 川島 宏一, 野村 名可男, 有田 智一, 鈴木 勉, EOM SUNYONG, BPGI関係教員

授業概要

This practical training enhances skills of problem formation and solution development through PBL (Problem Based Learning) to attack global issues in the field of “Earth Environment” and “Risk and Security”.
Firstly, students will set individual study project related to global issue. Then, students will conduct their research activities including literature review, data analysis, and survey by consulting with their advisor and mentor. Student will present on their study project and have discussion. This course also provide the lectures of methodology for data analysis related to themes of study project.

備考

Lectures are conducted in English. Students must follow this course at the same time as or after having accomplished the Seminars on Global Issues A-II (VB20022).

授業方法

学位プログラム・コンピテンスとの関係

It is essential to apply knowledge to attack global issues in broad areas. Through this practical training, students will obtain actual experiences to investigate some areas that had serious environmental incidents and make proposals to mitigate further risks.

授業の到達目標（学修成果）

Students should acquire skills for finding problems, analyzing data, and suggesting solution to mitigate further risks of global issues. In addition Students are expected to exercise their ability to express their views in discussions and debate.

キーワード

environment, risk, Security, disaster, global issue, sustainable development
