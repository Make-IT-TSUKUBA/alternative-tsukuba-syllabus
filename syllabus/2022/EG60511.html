EG60511   Practical Plant Biotechnology

1.0 単位, 3・4 年次, 春AB 木5
松倉 千昭, 江面 浩, 有泉 亨

授業概要

Plant cell, tissue and organ cultures for crop improvement will be introduced as conventional biotechnologies. The current status of of genetically modified (GM) crops and the genome editing technology will be introduced.

備考

Same as EC31231 and EG60021. A English Program Student who had taken EG60021 is not allowed.
英語で授業。
その他の実施形態

授業方法

講義

学位プログラム・専門コンピテンスとの関係

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

Acquisition of knowledge on plant tissue culture, genetically modified crops, plant molecular breeding, and use of biological genetic resource information.

キーワード

Plant biotechnology, tissue culture, genetically modified crop, plant genome science

授業計画

第1回 General introduction/Basic of Plant Tissue culture (Matsukura)
第2回 Mutant breeding and somaclonal variation (Matsukura)
第3回 Embryo/Ovule culture (Ezura)
第4回 Virus-free and micro-propagation (Ezura)
第5回 How to generate transgenic plants (Matsukura)
第6回 Sequencing technologies (Ariizumi)
第7回 Emergence of genome editing technology (Ariizumi)
第8回 Current status of GM-crop production in the world (Matsukura)
第9回 Evaluation and regulation of GM-crops and foods (Matsukura)
第10回 Current issues on development of GM-crops (Matsukura)

履修条件

成績評価方法

Basically, we assign a report to you every week and you are to submit it through the manaba by the next class.
The final exam will not be held. In order to be evaluated, report submission of 70% or more is required. We will comprehensively evaluate the submitted reports basing on their quality.
