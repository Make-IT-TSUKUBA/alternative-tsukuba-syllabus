Introduction to One-Planet Relational Studies in Literature and Culture:
“One-Planet Relational Studies in Literature and Culture” offers a new approach to research in literature and culture for students who are interested in the future of our planet, in understanding our globally shared problems and in finding creative solutions. The course focuses on global crises issues such as Corona Virus Pandemic, Global Warming, Aging Society, Refugee and Migration, Gender Equality, Discrimination, Poverty, Plastic Pollution, and Hunger, and reviews counteractive concepts such as resiliency, sustainability, mindfulness, and stewardship. The course is taught in English.

キーワード

One-Planet Relational Studies, Literature and Culture, Global Crises, Corona Virus Pandemic, Sustainability and Resiliency, Mindfulness and Stewardship

授業計画

This introductory class will begin by explaining the concept of “One-Planet Relational Studies in Literature and Culture”. It will then discuss and use the current Corona Virus Pandemic to introduce some of the basic tools and theories of our approach to understanding global crises in a literary and cultural perspective, in particular: basic concepts such as “discourse” and “signification”, “gender” and “power”, "agency", and point out some internationally developed approaches such as global and local responses to the Corona Virus Pandemic, “SDG” (Sustainable Development Goals) and NGO and art projects. The class will also provide the necessary know-how for academic research, writing and presentation skills. It will furthermore offer various concrete examples of how to do “One-Planet Relational Studies” analyses in literature and culture.
Syllabus is open to changes.

第1回 Introduction to "One-Planet Relational Studies in Literature and Culture"
第2回 Corona Virus Pandemic: responses in science, politics, society and culture
