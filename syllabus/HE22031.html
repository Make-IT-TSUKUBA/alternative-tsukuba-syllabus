授業概要

TOEFL ITPの得点力向上に役立つリスニングのトレーニングを行う。さらに、コミュニケーションスキルや英語一般の理解力の向上も目指す。

備考

医療科学類学生に限る。4B209で開講予定。
英語で授業。

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

At the end of this course, students will be able to
1. apply a specific set of listening skills to improve their TOEFL ITP scores
2. engage more effectively in short exchanges with native speakers of English
3. have a better understanding of vocabulary (phrasal verbs and idioms) and grammar (IF sentences)

キーワード

TOEFL English, Phrases for basic conversation

授業計画

Lessons will be carried out in-person. A set of 17 listening skills will be introduced over 8 weeks, with 2 mock listening tests carried out during the first lesson (pre-test) and last lesson (post-test). A typical lesson will proceed according to the following format: Explanations => Practice => Pair-work => Feedback, with the remaining time devoted to Q&A.
There is no final exam but students who miss the post-test with a valid reason (due to being sick etc.) will be required to take this test separately during the exam week.

履修条件

成績評価方法

Grades are based on work throughout the course. The grade will be calculated as a total of your
1. pre-test score (max. 10: CEFR A1=5, CEFR A2=7, CEFR B1=8, CEFR B2=9, CEFR C1=10)
2. post-test score (max. 40: CEFR A1=20, CEFR A2=25, CEFR B1=30, CEFR B2=35, CEFR C1=40)
3. assignment grade (6 assignments * 7 = max. 42)
4. class participation (max. 8: actively responding to questions (=choosing an answer) and engaging in pair-work)
* Students who miss the pre-test or the post-test with a valid reason (due to being sick etc.) will be given the opportunity to take these tests separately during the exam week.

学修時間の割り当て及び授業外における学修方法

In-Class learning: 15 hours
Out-of-Class learning: 30 hours
Self-study will involve reviewing the materials uploaded AFTER each class, checking unknown vocabulary, confirming understanding of lesson contents, practicing using the free tests specified below, and (if necessary) communicating (by email or in-person) with the instructor to clarify difficult points.
