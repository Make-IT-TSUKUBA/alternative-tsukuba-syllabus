EG60411   Biomaterial Science

1.0 単位, 3・4 年次, 秋AB 火2
江前 敏晴, 中川 明子, 小幡谷 英一

授業概要

Fundamentals and applications of paper science and papermaking engineering will be provided and they cover chemical structures of polysaccharides constituting fibers, pulping methods for extracting fibers from wood, papermaking technology such as beating, forming, calendering and coating, and geometrical, mechanical, optical, water-related properties of paper as well as biomass plastics to replace petroleum- resourced plastics and latest research topics.

Specific competence 1. Systematic expertise on Bioresource Science (Agricultural Science)

授業の到達目標(学修成果)

How to refine wood to obtain fibers in the form to apply to papermaking with ease will be understood. What kind of processing is required to obtain good properties of paper and paper properties required for specialized use will be also taught as well as new paper application to create sensors and electronics and paper conservation.

キーワード

Electronics, Fiber, Paper, Pulp, Sensor, Carbon emission
